XCharts

A charting and data visualization library for Unity.

XCharts Tutorial - 5min ⏱️

Easy to Use

XCharts is committed to providing Unity developers and creatives with a powerful yet simple-to-use chart library for quickly implementing a variety of chart effects.

Powerful

XCharts supports line, bar, pie, radar, scatter, heatmap, ring, candlestick, polar, parallel coordinates,3d line, 3d pie, 3d bar, 3d pyramid, funnel, gauge, liquid, pictorialbar, gantt, treemap, sankey and graph chart.

Configurable

XCharts offers a wealth of components and configuration options, supporting customization of chart effects and expansion of chart types through various aspects such as configuration and code.
